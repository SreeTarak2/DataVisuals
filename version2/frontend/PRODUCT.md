# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Mid-market data teams, BI managers, and operations leads who need accurate AI analytics without losing tribal knowledge. They're tired of 80%-accurate AI tools that hallucinate on domain-specific terms. Their context: evaluating tools while juggling existing reporting cycles. They need to trust the output before they buy in.

## Product Purpose

Signal is a context-aware AI analytics platform that captures business knowledge automatically as a byproduct of use. Every correction teaches the system; every insight compounds. It solves the tribal-knowledge problem that makes traditional AI BI tools plateau at 80% accuracy.

## Brand Personality

Clean, Approachable, Professional. Signal is the tool that makes data feel human — precise enough for analysts, clear enough for executives. Warm without being casual; expert without being cold.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS-cream templates with pastel gradients
- Overused glassmorphism and decorative blurs
- Cute mascots or playful illustrations (undermines trust)
- Dense, information-dashboard layouts on a marketing page
- The "hero-metric" template (big number + small label, repeated)

## Design Principles

- **Clarity First**: Every section answers one question. No decoration without purpose.
- **Warm Precision**: The interface is sharp but not cold. Amber warmth on a dark canvas signals both rigor and approachability.
- **Expert Confidence**: Quiet enough to let the data lead; authoritative enough to inspire trust. The page demonstrates the product, not just describes it.
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Dashboard mockups, live-feeling metrics, and product previews replace feature bullet lists.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance
- High-contrast text on dark backgrounds (≥4.5:1 body, ≥3:1 large text)
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` throughout
- Amber (#D89B14) never used for body text — accent only
